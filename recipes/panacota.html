Coconut Panacota

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup coconut milk
  2. 1/2 cup sugar
  3. 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  4. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. 1/4 cup heavy cream
  6. 1/2 cup coconut flakes

Steps

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • In a large bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and heavy cream.
  • Gradually add the coconut flakes and stir well until well combined.
  • Pour the batter into a greased 9x13 inch pan and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Remove from the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
