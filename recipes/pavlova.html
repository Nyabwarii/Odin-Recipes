Pavlova

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  2. 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  3. 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  4. 1 egg
  5. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  7. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  8. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  9. 1 cup milk
  10. 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  11. 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  12. 2 large eggs
  13. 1/2 cup heavy cream

Steps

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, and vanilla extract.
  • Add the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt, and stir well to combine.
  • Add the eggs and milk, and stir well to combine.
  • Gradually add the vegetable oil and cream cheese, stirring until well combined.
  • Pour the batter into a greased 9x13 inch pan, and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
